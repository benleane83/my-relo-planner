# Furniture

## Overview
Plan for furniture in London — what to ship from Dubai, what to sell, and what to buy new in the UK.

## Key Decision: Ship vs Buy New
- **Voltage**: UAE uses 220V with UK-style plugs (Type G) — same as UK, so most electronics are compatible
- **Furniture sizes**: UAE furniture tends to be large — London flats are significantly smaller
- **Shipping cost**: ~AED 8,000-15,000 depending on volume — may not be worth it for heavy/bulky items
- **Recommendation**: Ship only sentimental/high-value compact items. Buy larger furniture new in UK.

## Items to Ship
- Office desk (120cm x 60cm x 74cm) — good size for London home office
- 2x office chairs — ergonomic, worth keeping
- Breville Barista Pro coffee machine — same plug type, works in UK
- Personal items, books, kitchenware
- Artwork and decorative items

## Items to Sell in Dubai
- Large sofa set — too bulky for London flat
- Dining table (8-seater) — London flat will need smaller 4-seater
- King bed frame — different sizes and styles, buy fresh
- Large wardrobes — most London rentals come with built-in wardrobes
- Washing machine and fridge — rentals in UK usually include white goods

## Notes
- Most UK rental flats come partially furnished — confirm with landlord before buying
- IKEA delivers within 3-7 days in London — good for quick setup
- Made.com has 4-8 week lead times — order early if choosing them
- Measure the flat dimensions before ordering anything
