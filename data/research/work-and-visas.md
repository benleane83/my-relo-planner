# Work and Visas

## Overview
Visa and work arrangements for relocating from Dubai to the UK. Mark is transferring with his employer; Lisa will enter on a dependent visa.

## Mark — Intra-Company Transfer (ICT) Visa
- **Type**: Skilled Worker visa (previously ICT route, now folded into Skilled Worker)
- **Sponsor**: Current employer's UK entity
- **Status**: Application submitted, biometrics appointment 20th April
- **Processing time**: 3-8 weeks (priority service available for extra £500)
- **Duration**: Up to 5 years, renewable
- **Key requirements**:
  - Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) from employer ✅ Received
  - Minimum salary threshold: £38,700 or going rate for role (whichever higher)
  - English language requirement: exempt for ICT route
  - TB test certificate: required for UAE applicants ✅ Done at approved clinic
- **Start date in London**: 1st August 2026

## Lisa — Dependent Visa
- **Type**: Skilled Worker dependent (partner)
- **Can apply**: Once Mark's visa is approved
- **Requirements**:
  - Proof of relationship (marriage certificate)
  - Financial requirement (met through Mark's salary)
  - TB test certificate
  - Biometrics appointment
- **Work rights**: Full unrestricted work rights in the UK on dependent visa
- **Processing time**: Same 3-8 weeks

## UAE Exit Process
- Mark's UAE work visa will be cancelled by employer upon resignation/transfer
- Lisa's dependent visa is linked to Mark's — automatically cancelled
- **Grace period**: 30 days from visa cancellation to leave UAE
- **Important**: Time the visa cancellation to align with departure date
- End of service gratuity: Calculate based on years of service (UAE Labour Law)

## Post-Arrival Admin
- Apply for National Insurance (NI) number — needed for tax and benefits
- Register for Self Assessment if needed
- Update HMRC with tax residency change
- Check Double Taxation Agreement between UAE and UK (UAE has no income tax)

## Notes
- UK has no tax on worldwide income earned before becoming UK tax resident
- Split-year treatment may apply for the tax year of arrival (2026/27)
- Consider consulting a UK tax advisor for the transition year
- BRP (Biometric Residence Permit) will be collected from a Post Office after arrival
