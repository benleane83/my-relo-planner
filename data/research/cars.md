# Cars

## Overview
Plan for selling the car in Dubai and deciding whether to buy/lease a car in London.

## Selling Car in Dubai
- **Current vehicle**: 2022 Toyota RAV4, ~45,000 km
- **Estimated value**: AED 85,000-95,000
- **Options**:
  - **Dubizzle** — private sale, higher price but takes longer. List 6 weeks before departure.
  - **Cars24 / SellAnyCar** — instant offer, typically 10-15% less than private. Quick and easy.
  - **Dealer trade-in** — Al Futtaim or similar. Usually lowest offer but hassle-free.
- **Action items**:
  - Clear any outstanding traffic fines (check Dubai Police app)
  - Get car valuation from multiple sources
  - Cancel insurance and get refund for remaining months
  - Transfer or cancel Salik (toll) account

## UK Car Situation
- **Key question**: Do we even need a car in London?
- London has excellent public transport (Tube, Overground, buses)
- Oyster / contactless is very affordable — monthly cap ~£200 for zones 1-3
- If renting in Putney/Richmond, a car would be nice for weekend trips
- **Recommendation**: Don't buy immediately. Use public transport for first 3 months, then decide.

## If We Do Get a UK Car
| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| Lease (PCP) | Low upfront, swap every 3 years | Mileage limits, not an asset |
| Used purchase | Own outright, no restrictions | Depreciation, MOT/maintenance |
| Car subscription (e.g. Onto) | Flexible monthly, all-inclusive | Expensive long-term |

## Driving License
- UAE driving license can be exchanged for a UK license without a test (designated country)
- Apply via DVLA after arrival — need proof of UK address
- Process takes 2-3 weeks
- Can drive on UAE license for up to 12 months in the UK

## Notes
- Parking in London is expensive — £100-200/month for a resident permit depending on borough
- Congestion charge (£15/day) applies in central London
- ULEZ covers all of Greater London — need a compliant vehicle
