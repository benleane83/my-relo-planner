# Shipping and Freight

## Overview
Research into international shipping options from Dubai to London. Comparing costs, timelines, and providers.

## Key Questions
- Full Container Load (FCL) vs Groupage (shared container)?
- What's the optimal ship date to arrive close to our move-in?
- Customs duties on personal effects entering the UK?

## Shipping Options Comparison
| Option | Volume | Transit Time | Est. Cost (AED) | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Groupage (LCL) | 3-5 CBM | 6-10 weeks | 3,000-5,000 | Shared container, slower, cheaper |
| 20ft FCL | Up to 33 CBM | 3-4 weeks | 12,000-18,000 | Dedicated container, faster |
| Air freight | Up to 500kg | 5-7 days | 8,000-15,000 | Very fast, very expensive, small volume |

**Recommendation**: Groupage (LCL) — we're only shipping ~4 CBM of items (office equipment, personal items, kitchenware). FCL is overkill.

## Customs & Import Duties (UK)
- **Personal effects** (used items, owned 6+ months): **No duty or VAT** under Transfer of Residence relief (ToR1)
- Must apply for ToR1 relief via HMRC before or within 12 months of arrival
- Need to provide inventory list, proof of residence in UAE, and UK visa
- **New items** purchased specifically for the move: Standard VAT (20%) may apply
- **Prohibited items**: Certain food products, plants, some medicines — check HMRC list

## Insurance
- Transit insurance recommended — covers damage, loss, theft during shipping
- Allied Pickfords offers: ~AED 400 for AED 20,000 coverage
- Third-party option: Insure Your Move (UK company) — competitive rates
- **Action**: Get insurance quote alongside shipping quote
