# Shipping and Freight

## Overview
Research into international shipping options for our stuff.

## Key Questions
- Full Container Load vs Less than Container Load shipping
- What dates to send shipment for optimal arrival timing in Perth house

## Notes

New furniture shipped to Australia has an added 15.5% fees on value of goods and shipping
Existing LCL shipment was 3.25 CBM for boxes, and cost around $2500 AUD
Full Container Loads take 4-6 weeks to arrive, vs LCL taking up to 14 weeks
FCL shipment costs around 20,000 AED ($8k AUD)

Recommended removalists are Santa Fe, Allied Pickfords or Seven Seas (currently not shipping from UAE)
