## Notes

1. Rabies vaccination administered
2. At 3-4 weeks following the rabies vaccination, you will need to complete the Rabies blood titre test which will need to be sent to an approved lab for testing. From the date the blood sample is received at the lab, the 180-day waiting period will commence, during this time we can continue with the process to prepare your pet for export.
3. Apply for the RNATT declaration which must be completed by the exporting countries government vet. – Sandy Paws will complete this
4. Apply for an Australian import permit (these are currently taking around 2 months to be issued, however this time is within the 180 day wait period) – Jetpets will arrange this
5. Making initial reservation with quarantine for the team to review and approve. – Jetpets will arrange this
6. Pre-travel vet work to commence 8 weeks before departure date for dogs – Sandy Paws will complete this
7. Final endorsements – Sandy Paws will complete this
8. International flight to Melbourne – Sandy Paws will arrange this
9. 10 days quarantine, Mickleham facility – Jetpets will arrange this
10. Collection from quarantine, domestic transport within Australia – Jetpets will arrange this
