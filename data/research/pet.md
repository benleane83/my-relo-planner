# Pet Relocation

## Overview
Requirements and logistics for relocating Snoopy (dog) from Dubai to the UK under the UK PETS Travel Scheme.

## UK PETS Scheme Requirements
The UK has specific entry requirements for pets from non-EU countries (including UAE):

1. **Microchip** — ISO 11784/11785 compliant, 15-digit ✅ Done (confirmed readable)
2. **Rabies vaccination** — Must be given after microchip. Booster given 15th March 2026 ✅ Done
3. **Rabies blood titre test** — NOT required for UK entry from UAE (only required for unlisted countries)
4. **Animal Health Certificate (AHC)** — Must be issued by an official government vet within 10 days of travel
5. **Tapeworm treatment** — Must be administered by a vet 1-5 days before arrival in UK. Record on AHC.

## Travel Logistics
- Snoopy is too large for cabin travel — must go as **manifest cargo**
- **Emirates SkyCargo** — flies pets as cargo, good reputation, direct DXB→LHR
- **British Airways** — also offers pet cargo, IAG Cargo handles it
- **IATA-approved crate** — purchased from Pet Zone Dubai (AED 320). Snoopy is being crate-trained.
- Arrival point: **Heathrow Animal Reception Centre (HARC)** — open 24/7, collect within 4 hours

- Book cargo space early — limited pet slots per flight, especially in summer
- Snoopy should not eat 4-6 hours before flight but have water available
